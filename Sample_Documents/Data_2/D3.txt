Natural Language Processing (NLP) plays a crucial role in document management. It enables tasks like text extraction, document summarization, and sentiment analysis. NLP techniques help in understanding the semantics and context of documents. Document management systems store, retrieve, and organize documents efficiently. They provide features like version control, access control, and full-text search. NLP algorithms are integrated into document management systems to improve user experience and productivity. These algorithms automate repetitive tasks and assist users in finding relevant information. Document management systems are widely used in organizations for managing digital documents and workflows. NLP-powered document management systems streamline document handling and facilitate collaboration among users.
